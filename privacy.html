Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

What We Collect

Creator OS collects social media analytics data (follower counts, video views, likes, comments) from platforms you choose to connect, including TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter/X. This data is collected solely through official platform APIs after you explicitly authorize access.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to display analytics and performance insights on your personal Creator OS dashboard. We use this data to help you make informed decisions about your content strategy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties. Your analytics data is only visible to you when logged into your dashboard.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

Creator OS integrates with TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter/X APIs. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing the data they provide. We only access data you explicitly authorize.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: creatorosapp@gmail.com
